SHIPPING POLICY


Mystic Jewels offers free shipping on all orders. For total orders below Rs.999, Rs.55 is charged. COD charges of Rs.45 as convenience charges are applied to all cash on delivery orders. Shipping duration usually takes one week but may take longer, depending upon your location.
Customers usually receive their packages within 3 to 5 working days, once they are shipped. This delivery period may be longer for more remote locations. If you have any questions about the expected delivery period, please call us at +91 9890-097-996 or email us at support@Mystic Jewels Mobile App

Returns / Refund Policy

What is your policy on returns?
If the Mystic Jewels product received by you is in a damaged or defective condition, you may return the Mystic Jewels product to us within 14 days of receipt, and we will provide a replacement of the product, No refund of the product will be Provided. In all cases, you must return the Mystic Jewels unused with a copy of the dispatch note. Return costs are not pre-paid. Postage or other return costs will be the customer's responsibility. You must return the goods, together with their packaging, in the same condition as supplied to you. Returns are sent at your risk.
